About

My name's Jonathon Marlar. Everyone calls me Cole.

I am a web developer and user interface specialist.

I was born in Corinth, Mississippi and grew up here in the Shoals area. Growing up I loved video games and all things electronic. My interest in computers and video games led to my journey to be a programmer and UI developer.

I attained a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science degree from the University of North Alabama in December 2015 with a minor in Human Computer Interaction and User Experience. While I was there, I worked with the Quality Enhancement Plan (QEP) Office to manage content for the Undergraduate Research webpage on UNA's website, as well as make a full website for the Alabama Geographic Alliance.
